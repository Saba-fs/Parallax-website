Introducing the Maha Indian Festivals

Living for those festival moments that take our breath away.

Holi

Known as the festival of Colors, and people throw colored powder on each other to create a magical, iridescent glow.

Eid al-fitr

Known as the festival of breaking the fast

Onam

A harvest festival that marks the end of the harvest season.

Dussehra

The festival that celebrates the victory of good over evil, truth over falsehood, and righteousness over injustice

Diwali

Known as the Festival of Lights,which symbolizes the victory of light over darkness.

Christmas

The festival marks the celebration of the birth anniversary of Jesus Christ.
